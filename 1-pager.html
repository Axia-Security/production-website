The Judgment Layer for Insider Risk

Your DLP generates alerts. Your IRM generates dashboards. Axia generates decisions.

The Problem

Insider risk is the most expensive, hardest-to-detect threat in cybersecurity.

  • $16.2M average cost per incident (Ponemon 2025)
  • 70-90% of DLP alerts are false positives
  • 86 days average containment time (IBM 2025)
  • 85% involve employees with legitimate access
  • 90% of data theft happens before departure

Detection without understanding is just noise.

The Axia Approach

Judgment over Enforcement

Contextual AI determines what is truly risky, not just what matches a pattern.

Engagement over Alerts

Coaches employees, notifies managers, escalates to security. Right person, right time.

Understanding WHY

Every case explains what happened, why it matters, why it is unusual.

Human-in-the-Loop

AI handles triage. Humans confirm critical actions. Intelligent partnership.
