Version 1.0 · Last updated: 2026-06-26

1. Who we are (Controller)

This policy is issued by Axia Security Inc., 266 Tenafly Road, Tenafly, NJ 07670, USA ("AXIA"), the controller of record. AXIA provides an insider-risk-management software product. This policy explains how AXIA handles personal data in two distinct contexts:

  • Context A — our website and business operations, where AXIA acts as a controller of personal data about website visitors, prospects, and business contacts; and
  • Context B — the AXIA product as deployed for our customers, where AXIA acts, at most, as a processor on the customer's behalf and, by design, handles little or no customer personal data directly.

Privacy / data-protection contact: security@axiasecurity.io.

2. Context A — Personal data we process through our website and business operations

We keep our website deliberately data-minimal. axiasecurity.io does not set cookies and does not run any web analytics. We do not use advertising pixels or behavioral tracking. (If we add cookies or analytics in the future, we will update this policy at that time.)

The only personal data we collect through the website is:

What we collect When Purpose Legal basis (GDPR Art. 6 / UK GDPR / Swiss FADP)
Demo-booking details (your name, email, and the scheduling information you provide) When you book a demo via our scheduling link (calendly.com/axia-demo) To schedule and hold a product demo and respond to your request Art. 6(1)(b) (steps prior to entering a contract) and/or Art. 6(1)(f) (legitimate interest in responding to enquiries)
Email correspondence (your email address and the contents of your message) When you email us To respond to and manage your enquiry Art. 6(1)(f) (legitimate interest)

Processors we use in Context A: - Calendly — processes the booking data (name, email, scheduling details) you submit when you schedule a demo.

We do not sell personal data, and we do not carry out automated decision-making producing legal or similarly significant effects in Context A.

3. Context B — Personal data in the AXIA product (as deployed for customers)

AXIA's product is deployed inside the customer's own controlled environment. AXIA does not offer a SaaS deployment, and the language model is always customer-provided (a cloud LLM the customer contracts for, or a customer-managed/approved LLM endpoint); AXIA never hosts the model. As a result:

  • Customer data resides and is processed only within the customer's own environment / cloud tenant, with no egress to AXIA-controlled infrastructure for inference, logging, telemetry, or training. AXIA, by design, does not custody customer personal data in the standard deployment.
  • The cloud infrastructure provider and the foundation-model service used for inference are the customer's own direct counterparties, not AXIA sub-processors.
  • AXIA does not use customer data to train, fine-tune, adapt, or otherwise enhance any AI model, and maintains model isolation across deployments.
  • AXIA may retain only de-identified / aggregated information derived from customer data for product-improvement and statistical purposes, under conditions that prevent re-identification. For this reason AXIA does not represent that "all data is destroyed" — non-identifiable derivatives may be retained.

Where AXIA processes any personal data on a customer's behalf, it does so as a processor under the customer's instructions and the parties' Data Processing Addendum. The customer is the controller for that data and is the primary point of contact for the rights of its own data subjects (e.g., the customer's employees). Cross-border transfer mechanisms, where relevant, are addressed in the Data Processing Addendum on a per-deal basis.

4. International transfers

AXIA's controller of record, Axia Security Inc., is based in the United States (New Jersey); our website host (Wix), our scheduling provider (Calendly), and our email provider (Google Workspace) may process data in other jurisdictions. Where personal data of individuals in the EEA, the UK, or Switzerland is transferred outside those areas, we rely on appropriate safeguards (such as our providers' standard contractual clauses / data-processing terms).

5. Retention

  • Context A: demo-booking and correspondence data are retained for as long as needed to handle your enquiry and our ongoing business relationship, and are then deleted or anonymized.
  • Context B: governed by the customer Data Processing Addendum; customer data remains in the customer's environment under the customer's own retention controls, and AXIA retains only de-identified derivatives.

6. Your rights and how to exercise them

Depending on your jurisdiction (GDPR / UK GDPR / Swiss FADP / Israeli Privacy Protection Law), you may have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict, or object to our processing of your personal data, and to data portability.

How to exercise: contact us at security@axiasecurity.io. We respond to data-subject requests within 30 days of receipt (extendable where permitted by law for complex requests).

  • For Context A data (our website and business contacts), AXIA is the controller — contact us directly.
  • For Context B data (data within a customer's deployment), the customer is the controller — please direct your request to the relevant customer; we will support the customer as their processor.

Right to complain: you may lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority — for example, your EU member-state Data Protection Authority, the UK Information Commissioner's Office (ICO), or the Swiss Federal Data Protection and Information Commissioner (FDPIC).

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in our website, our providers, or applicable law. The version and date appear at the top. This is Version 1.0.
