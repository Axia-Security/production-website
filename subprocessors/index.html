AXIA Sub-processors

Version 1.0 · Last updated: 2026-06-30

1. Product deployment — no sub-processors with access to customer data

The AXIA product is deployed inside the customer's own controlled environment (on-premises or the customer's own cloud tenant). AXIA does not offer a SaaS deployment, and the language model is always customer-provided (a cloud LLM the customer contracts for, or a customer-managed/approved endpoint); AXIA never hosts the model. As a result:

  • Customer production data resides and is processed only within the customer's own environment, with no egress to AXIA-controlled infrastructure for inference, logging, telemetry, or training.
  • The cloud infrastructure provider and the foundation-model service used for inference are the customer's own direct counterparties — they are not AXIA sub-processors.
  • Accordingly, in the standard (and only) product deployment model, AXIA engages no sub-processors that process or have access to customer production data.
